📊 Customer Behaviour Data Analysis :-

📌 Project Overview :

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior data to uncover insights related to purchasing patterns, customer segmentation, and revenue trends. The analysis is performed using SQL, python and visualized using Power BI.

🎯 Objectives :-

1) Analyze customer spending behavior

2) Identify top-performing products

3) Understand the impact of discounts and subscriptions

4) Segment customers based on purchase history

5) Compare revenue across demographics (gender, age group)

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used :-

1)SQL (MySQL)

2)Power BI

3)Microsoft Excel

4)Python (Pandas).

📊 Key SQL Analysis

1. Revenue by Gender

Analyzed total revenue generated by male and female customers.

2. High Spending Customers with Discounts

Identified customers who used discounts but still spent above average.

3. Top Rated Products

Found top 5 products based on average review rating.

4. Shipping Type Analysis

Compared average purchase amount between Standard and Express shipping.

5. Subscription Impact

Analyzed whether subscribed customers spend more than non-subscribers.

6. Discount Effectiveness

Calculated percentage of purchases with discounts for each product.

7. Customer Segmentation

Segmented customers into:

New

Returning

Loyal

8. Top Products by Category

Identified top 3 most purchased products in each category.

9. Repeat Buyer Analysis

Checked if repeat buyers are more likely to subscribe.

10. Revenue by Age Group

Segmented customers into age groups and analyzed revenue contribution.

📈 Power BI Dashboard Features

Revenue by Gender (Bar Chart)

Top Products by Rating (Bar Chart)

Discount Analysis (%)

Customer Segmentation (Pie Chart)

Subscription vs Revenue (Table/Matrix)

Age Group Revenue Analysis

🔍 Key Insights :-

Certain products consistently receive higher ratings

Discounted purchases significantly influence buying behavior

Loyal customers contribute a major portion of revenue

Subscription status impacts overall spending

Specific age groups contribute more to total revenue
